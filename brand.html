Google Play — SteadiDay assets.

The two assets Google Play Console requires: a 512×512 developer icon (your existing SteadiDay mark, cleaned up and exported to spec), and a 1024×500 feature graphic that runs at the top of the store listing.

Spec checklist

Google Play Console requirements

App icon (developer icon)

  • 512 × 512 px
  • 32-bit PNG (with alpha)
  • Maximum file size: 1 MB
  • No transparency in the visible area — Play renders it on a tile

Feature graphic

  • 1024 × 500 px
  • JPG or 24-bit PNG (no transparency)
  • Maximum file size: 15 MB
  • No text inside the inner ~924 × 400 safe area is guaranteed visible

Tip

  • Avoid putting critical text near the right edge — Play may overlay a "Play" button there.
