About

The firm behind the work.

SCM Solutions LLC is a healthcare consulting firm founded on the belief that the best consulting comes from people who have led the work directly — inside the organizations, alongside the teams, accountable for the outcomes.

The firm draws on 8+ years of experience across large multi-hospital health systems, integrated health plans, Fortune 25 managed care organizations, national pharmacy benefits companies, and regional Medicaid plans. That experience spans population health, quality, pharmacy operations, health equity, clinical workflow optimization, and large-scale organizational change.

SCM Solutions also brings practical experience applying AI tools to build real products — not as a buzzword, but as a hands-on capability used to design, develop, and ship a consumer health application from concept to the App Store.
