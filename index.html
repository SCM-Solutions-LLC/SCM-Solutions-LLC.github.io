About

Background

SCM Solutions LLC is a Virginia-based healthcare consulting firm founded on a simple idea: the best consulting comes from people who have actually led the work, not just advised on it.

The firm draws on 8+ years of direct experience leading enterprise programs inside large multi-hospital health systems, integrated health plans, Fortune 25 managed care organizations, national pharmacy benefits companies, and regional Medicaid plans — managing complex portfolios spanning population health, quality, pharmacy operations, health equity, EHR-related initiatives, and large-scale organizational change.

That experience includes leading operational readiness for multi-hospital acquisitions, standing up social determinants of health screening workflows, directing mass vaccination operations serving thousands of patients daily, and managing technology implementations that required deep coordination between clinical, operational, and IT teams.

SCM Solutions also develops digital health products. SteadiDay, a daily companion app for adults 50+, was built to address a gap in the market for genuinely accessible health technology.

Our Product

SteadiDay

In addition to consulting, SCM Solutions develops digital health products. SteadiDay is an all-in-one daily companion app built for adults 50 and older — bringing medication management, task organization, health tracking, safety features, and everyday utilities into one straightforward interface.

Medication reminders, daily tasks, Apple Calendar sync, Emergency SOS, fall detection, Apple Health integration, food and water logging, mind games, magnifier, flashlight, and Find My iPhone access.
