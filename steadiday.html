Who it’s for

Built for the people who’ve been holding it all in their head.

SteadiDay isn’t a productivity app trying to be a health app. It’s a daily companion designed from the ground up around the rhythm of managing your health.

For people 50 and up

More medications. More appointments. More to remember. SteadiDay uses large type, big tap targets, and plain language — so the app does the remembering, and you can focus on the day.

For adult children helping a parent

Design Principles

Designed differently on purpose.

Four decisions that make SteadiDay feel different from the calendar, notes, and reminder apps already on your phone.

01 / Type

Larger by default.

Body text is 20pt or larger. Headings are bigger still. No squinting, no settings to dig through to make it readable.

02 / Tap

Big targets, every time.

Every button and every list item is at least 56 pixels tall. No tiny chevrons or accidental taps.

03 / Words

Plain language.

“Add a task.” “Browse templates.” Not “Discover.” Not “Explore.” The buttons say what they do.

04 / Privacy

Quiet by default.

No account. No login. No data leaves the device. Free to download, free to use — built as a service, not a funnel.

Privacy Promise

Your health information belongs to you.

SteadiDay was built by a healthcare program management practice, not an ad-tech company. That distinction matters. Everything you enter — tasks, medications, appointments, notes — stays on your device. It is used only to display your day to you. It is never sold, never combined with anyone else’s information, and never shared with advertisers or data brokers for any reason.

There is no account to create. There is no profile. There is no advertising network behind the app. SteadiDay is offered freely as a tool — that is the whole arrangement.

Safety Sessions & Live Location Sharing

When you start a Safety Session, SteadiDay uses your phone’s sensors to detect falls. If a fall is detected and you don’t respond, your trusted contact is alerted by SMS with a link to your live location so they can find you quickly.

During an active session your GPS coordinates are sent to our server and shared only with your designated emergency contacts via a secure, unique link. Here is exactly how it works:

  • Only the most recent location is stored — we do not build a location history or track your movements over time.
  • The live-location link is unique to each session and stops working the moment the session ends.
  • Your location is never shared with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party.
  • You can end the session at any time to stop sharing immediately, or turn off automatic sessions in Settings.
  • On iOS, your phone shows a visible indicator whenever background location is active. You can change location permissions at any time in your device Settings.

Outside of a Safety Session, no location data leaves your device.
